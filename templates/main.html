How Face Swapper Works


1 Upload Source Image

Click "Start face swapping" to enter the tool's face swap workflow. Hit "Add photo" to upload the source image that contains a face you will swap. Ensure the photo is of good quality.


2 Upload Target Image

You can then upload another photo as the target image. This means you will switch the header of the source image to the target image. Also, you can choose from the presets provided.


3 Select a part to Swap

Click "part" to show options of parts available for face swapping, then let the face swapper show its magic.


4 Start AI Face Swap

Click "Swap Face Now" to let the face swapper show its magic. It takes only 5-10 seconds to complete a face swap. Our face changer guarantees high-quality output without any watermarks.


5 Preview and Download

Bingo! Your face is swapped to who you like! Preview the final result on the same page. Click 'Download' to save the watermark-free picture to your device. Share it for fun!
